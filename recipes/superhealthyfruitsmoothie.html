Super Healthy Fruit Smoothie

Source: allrecipes.com

This healthy fruit smoothie with raspberries, blueberries, strawberries, and more is absolutely wonderful! I have it for breakfast every morning.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup fresh blueberries
  • ⅓ cup fresh raspberries
  • 4 large fresh strawberries, hulled
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • ⅓ cup pomegranate juice
  • ⅓ cup mango juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Preparation and Cooking Steps

  • Place berries into a blender with milk, pomegranate and mango juices, and honey. Cover and purée until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
